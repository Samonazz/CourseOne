Elemental Shaman

Elemental Shaman is a mail-wearing ranged DPS caster with many great spell effects and different flavours of abilities, wielding Nature, Fire and Frost spells to damage its opponents.

We can use staffs or one-handed maces with shields and, depending on our builds, switch between very sturdy and very mobile depending on the encounter.

As a class with a healing specialization in Restoration, we excel at off-healing our teammates and, in general, providing various utility with our totems.

Elemental Shaman is the spec to play for people who love playing a caster that can specialize in helping their teammates or just like to look at impressive spell effects.

How to play Elemental Shaman

This is a guide on how to use your rotations as an Elemental Shaman & what consumables you are supposed to use.

Rotation

Pre-cast Storm Elemental 3 seconds before pull. Ensure not to have any enemies targeted; otherwise, this might pull. Pre-cast Stormkeeper about 1.5 seconds before pull. Cast Primordial Wave to apply Flame Shock Cast Ascendance. Cast Lava Burst.

Openers for Lightning builds depend a lot on the amount of Maelstrom Maelstrom you can get to at different points. In other words, in a mythic+ pull, going into a fight with Maelstrom Maelstrom allows you to easily Surge of Power every Stormkeeper-empowered Lightning Bolt. The raid opener allows you to get Master of the Elements on one of these strong Lightning Bolts and Surge of Power on another. This currently seems to be the optimal way to perform the opener. Be aware that while Storm Elemental is not a "hostile" ability and would not put you into combat immediately, some trinkets have been known to proc on it regardless. This can easily lead to pre-pulls.
